About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, famously known as the City of Pearls, is a blend of tradition, technology, and history. It is the capital of Telangana, known for its stunning landmarks like Charminar, Golconda Fort, and the modern IT hub of Hitech City. From the grandeur of Chowmahalla Palace to the flavors of Hyderabadi Biryani, Hyderabad offers an unforgettable experience.

Top Attractions

Charminar

The most iconic landmark of Hyderabad, built in 1591.

Golconda Fort

A majestic fortress with a rich history and fantastic acoustics.

Birla Mandir

A beautiful white marble temple overlooking the city.

Hussain Sagar Lake

A scenic lake with a giant Buddha statue at the center.

Chowmahalla Palace

Once the seat of the Nizams, now a stunning historical attraction.
